We strive to ship orders as quickly as possible but please note that on occasion, there may be delays in dispatching if artwork is going viral.

Border Sizes?

The size listed in the product description is the final size of the artwork. The artwork itself is printed a bit smaller than the final product size.

Can I Add A Request At Checkout?

Many lovely collectors ask for special things at the checkout, such as signatures, extras and hand written notes from me. While this is wonderful and I would love to, I am simply no longer packaging prints personally in order to spend more time creating art and content for you to enjoy!

Requests will be noted, but cannot be guaranteed. This does not include requests to speed up dispatching times as we do not jump people ahead of the rest. Fairness always!ü•∞

Where Can I Find My Order Confirmation?

Your order confirmation and invoice is delivered to your inbox automatically after purchase. If you cannot find it in your primary inbox, it may have ended up in your spam folder!

Paper invoices are rolled with a sticker on top of protected prints are for our processing records. Sometimes the invoices can be ripped during the unboxing process, but not to worry! You have a digital invoice in your order confirmation for your recording convenience.

What Is The Best Way To Contact You?

If you have a query about your purchase, please be sure to submit a form through the contact page. I receive hundreds of DMs daily and if you are following up there, it is likely I may miss it!

The contact page is to keep everything nice and simple for my team, so we can get back to you as soon as possible.

Please use the contact page and not any email address you may find on this website or social media! Otherwise, we are unable to guarantee a response. Some emails are no longer active.

Can I Purchase Through My Currency?

You sure can! Prices are listed in Australian dollars but this will automatically convert to your currency after payment.

Will I Get A Certificate Of Authenticity?

Originals and limited editions are the only works that arrive with a certificate of authenticity.

Will My Print Be Hand-Signed?

Unfortunately, open edition prints do not arrive hand-signed. The original signature is on the fine art print but will never be available hand-signed. Only limited editions and originals are signed to preserve the integrity of works that increase in value over time.
